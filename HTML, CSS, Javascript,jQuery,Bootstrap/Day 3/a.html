What is Isha Foundation, Adiyogi?

Adiyogi Bengaluru is a spiritual destination located on the outskirts of the city, offering visitors a peaceful and rejuvenating environment. The 112-feet tall statue of Adiyogi Shiva, symbolizing inner transformation, stands as a striking landmark surrounded by scenic landscapes.
Managed by the Isha Foundation, the site also features a Yogic practice space and regular meditation programs. Visitors can engage in guided sessions and attend special events, especially during Mahashivratri, which draws thousands of devotees and spiritual seekers.
The calm atmosphere, well-maintained surroundings, and beautiful views make Adiyogi a must-visit for those seeking tranquility, reflection, or spiritual connection near Bengaluru.
