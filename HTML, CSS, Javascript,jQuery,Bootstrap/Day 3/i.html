ISKCON Temple

What is ISKCON Temple?

The ISKCON Temple in Bengaluru, located in Rajajinagar, is one of the largest ISKCON temples in the world. It is dedicated to Lord Krishna and Radha and serves as a major spiritual and cultural center in the city.
The temple features grand architecture with gold-plated kalashas and intricately designed interiors. Daily rituals, devotional songs, and cultural programs attract thousands of devotees and tourists alike.
Visitors can enjoy prasadam (sanctified food), attend bhajans, and explore the spiritual book stalls. The temple promotes the teachings of the Bhagavad Gita and emphasizes a peaceful, devotional lifestyle.
