What is Nandi Hills?

Nandi Hills, located about 60 km from Bengaluru, is a popular hill station known for its breathtaking sunrise views. Perched at an altitude of 1,478 meters, it offers a cool and serene escape from the city's hustle.
The hill is rich in history and was once the summer retreat of Tipu Sultan. Visitors can explore ancient temples, Tipuâ€™s Drop, and the fort walls that whisper tales of the past. The lush greenery and mist-covered trails make it a favorite among nature lovers.
Adventure seekers also enjoy trekking, cycling, and paragliding here. Nandi Hills promises peace, history, and scenic charm.
