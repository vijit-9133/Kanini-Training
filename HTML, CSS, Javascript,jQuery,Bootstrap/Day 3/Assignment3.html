About Bengaluru

Bengaluru, also known as Bangalore, is the capital city of Karnataka and one of India's major metropolitan hubs. Often referred to as the "Silicon Valley of India," the city is a leading center for information technology and innovation, attracting professionals from across the globe.
Beyond its tech industry, Bengaluru is known for its pleasant climate, green spaces, and vibrant culture. It’s home to numerous parks like Cubbon Park and Lalbagh, along with a thriving food, music, and art scene. The city seamlessly blends tradition and modernity.
With its rich history, diverse population, and dynamic lifestyle, Bengaluru offers something for everyone. Whether you're exploring historic temples, visiting tech campuses, or enjoying a cup of filter coffee at a local café, the city's charm is sure to leave a lasting impression.

Best time to visit

The best time to visit Bengaluru is during the cooler months from October to February. The weather during this period is pleasant, with temperatures ranging from 15°C to 28°C, making it ideal for sightseeing, garden walks, and outdoor activities.
These months also coincide with several cultural festivals and flower shows, especially in places like Lalbagh Botanical Garden. The city's parks, lakes, and attractions are at their most vibrant, and the clear skies make for beautiful sunrises and sunsets.
While Bengaluru has a moderate climate year-round, summer (March to May) can get warm, and the monsoon (June to September) might disrupt travel plans due to frequent showers.
