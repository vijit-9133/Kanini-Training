Mandaragiri Hills

What is Mandaragiri Hills?

Mandaragiri Hills, also known as Basadi Betta, is a serene and scenic destination located near Tumkur, Karnataka, about 60 km from Bengaluru. It is a popular spot for nature lovers, trekkers, and those seeking a peaceful weekend getaway.
The main attraction is the beautiful Jain temple complex at the top of the hill, offering panoramic views of the surrounding landscape. The climb is relatively easy, with around 400 well-laid steps.
